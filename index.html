Welcome To Spectra The Convention Center

The Perfect Venue for your next event

The Spectra venue is welcoming with neutral colors and hedges at the entrance. Inside, the neutral colors are offset by a white ceiling with dark herry colored wood beams that create parallel lines across the outside edge of the room. Perched on the hillside, large windows allow sweeping vistas of the Tri-Valley below. Double doors lead to an outdoor patio, with decorative light posts, roses and unobstructed views during the day with glimmering city lights visible at night.
